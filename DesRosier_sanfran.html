San Francisco: the best and the worst

Japantown

This is my favorite neighborhood in San Francisco. It is clean, there are lots of shops for paper and pens, karaoke bars, and lots of good ramen and Japanese restaurants.

The Marina

This is my least favorite neighborhood in San Francisco. The nightlife is absolute garbage. I had heard rumors about how awful it was, and was still surprised when I finally experienced it for myself. Without going into too much detail, a random chick tried to fight me by clawing at my face and neck. Lucky for me, my older brothers taught me how to really fight (hint: that's not it).
